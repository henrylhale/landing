My Tutoring Philosophy

I think it's most effective to sort the practice questions you miss into a taxonomy of error types that is intuitive to you. Then, you can learn to quickly run down that list to double-check those questions you're only 80% sure on. Every question has four wrong answers, so if you can identify just why they're wrong, you can be certain about the right one. I help you develop your own personalized classification that makes sense to you, then we work on practice questions that give you the opportunity to dodge your most common mistakes.
